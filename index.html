About Me

I am an automation engineer with deep expertise in industrial software development, Machine vision, and control systems architecture. My foundation began in manufacturing environments—where reliability, precision, and continuous operation are non-negotiable requirements. From there, I expanded into software development, focusing on C#, WPF, Machine vision systems, and hardware integration.

I don't approach programming with a "make it run" mentality, but rather with systems thinking: clean architecture, performance optimization, and long-term scalability. For industrial software, three pillars matter: operational stability, maintainability through change, and flexible expansion.

Engineering Approach

I dig into root causes rather than surface solutions. Deep analysis of runtime behavior, memory management, threading, resource cleanup. Prioritizing clarity, stability, and complete observability in debugging.

Philosophy & Vision

Clear code beats clever code. Performance measured by data. Systems scale without rewriting. Architecture outlives technology. Building independent vision systems with optimized inference, reusable frameworks, and true hardware integration.
